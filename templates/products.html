Leek Micros

Thin green leaves with tiny black seeds attached. Leeks are similar to scallions, onions or garlic. They have a mild onion-like taste, a bit less bitter than scallions. Leeks add color and flavor to any dish. A distinct taste and long-lasting aftertaste that goes well with many meal combinations, either as a central feature or side garnish.

Broccoli

Broccoli microgreens have a delicious mild, fresh broccoli flavor. They are super tasty and is an excellent choice as a base for any microgreen salad and can be a perfect compliment for egg salad, chicken salad, and other vegetable based dishes.

Kohlrabi: Purple Vienna

These micros can be used to add flavor, texture, and visual appeal to mixed salads. Purple Vienna yields medium to dark green leaves with stems that range from whitish purple to medium purple in color. Take advantage of their vibrant color to add visual appeal to any dish. Add just before serving, to avoid wilting the leaves and robbing the microgreens of their crunch.
