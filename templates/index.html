Our microgreens are grown hydroponically on coconut fiber pads. We grow on coconut fiber pads as opposed to soil so that we can deliver you a live product without creating a mess in the kitchen. Delivering you the live product ensures that product is literally as fresh as possible, maximizing nutrition. In addition extends the shelf life, simply put a little water in the container and the plants will continue to live.

You can harvest the plants on an as needed basis. To harvest our microgreens, use scissors or a knife to cut off the amount you need for your meal.

Once you've harvested, you're ready to eat. We recommend harvesting at meal time rather than all at once for maximum freshness!
